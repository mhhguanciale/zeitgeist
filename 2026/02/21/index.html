  • Fed Chair Transition: Markets are pricing a ~94% probability of Kevin Warsh being nominated as the next Fed Chair. This signals a potential shift toward a more market-sensitive, potentially politically aligned monetary policy later in 2026.

  • Investment Implication: The "no landing" scenario is the consensus for Q1. Avoid aggressive duration bets ($ZROZ/TLT) until yields test the 4.25%-4.4% range. Focus on quality equities that can withstand 4% yields for another quarter.
