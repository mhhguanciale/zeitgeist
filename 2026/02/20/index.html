  • Rate Outlook: Expect a "Pause-Pause-Pause" cadence for the immediate future. The market is pricing out aggressive cuts in Q1/Q2 2026 as inflation data remains sticky (FRED CPI trend shows a steady climb to ~332.8).

Geopolitics: The "War Premium" Returns
